**Partial Differential Equations**

# Partial Differential Equations

## Equations

!!! theorem: Heat Equation
    $$
        \begin{equation}
        U_t = \alpha^2 U_{xx}
        \end{equation}
    $$

!!! theorem: Wave Equation
    $$
        \begin{equation}
        U_{tt} = \alpha^2 U_{xx}
        \end{equation}
    $$

## Initial Bountary Problems (IBP)

!!! example: Initial Boundary
    $$
        \begin{align}
        &U_t = \alpha^2 U_{xx},  &= t > 0, 0 < x < L \\
        &U(0,t) = U(L,t)=0,      &= t > 0 \\
        &U(x,0) = f(x),          &= 0 < x < L
        \end{align}
    $$
