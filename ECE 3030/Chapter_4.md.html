**Chapter 4** # Excess Carriers ## Optical Excitation When a photon with $E = h\gamma \geq E_g$ strikes an electron in the valence band, the electron is excited into the conduction band. The excess energy from the photon will scatter into the lattice and cause the electron to decay back to the conduction band edge, $E_c$. Afterwards, the electron will recombine with the hole it created when it was hit with the photon.
